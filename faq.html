Frequently Asked Questions

How do I buy a plan?

All purchases are handled on our Discord. Click Open Ticket, choose your plan, and our staff will set you up.

What payment methods do you accept?

UPI and other region-friendly methods via Discord tickets. We’ll share secure payment instructions in the ticket.

Can I upgrade later?

Yes. We can upgrade your plan and migrate your data with minimal downtime. Message us in your ticket.

Do you provide backups?

Yes—each plan includes backups as listed. You can request restores via Discord.

Can I host any Minecraft edition?

Yes—Java and Bedrock. Share your version, modpack, and any special requirements in the ticket.

Do you offer development services?

Yes! We build Discord bots/servers and full MC server setups. See Plans or contact us.

Where are your servers located?

We deploy based on demand for best latency. Ask in Discord for current regions and availability.

Refunds?

Please review our Terms of Service. Refunds are handled case-by-case via support tickets.
